about me

photo of Asen Velichkov

Hello there! I am Asen Velichkov, a freelance web designer and developer from Bulgaria, currently working from Finland. After many years in project management I decided to follow my passion and dedicated all of my time to web design and development. I like it a lot, because it's a nice way to constantly think, learn and create. And, after all, you even get paid for it!

my code

Minimalism

because less is more

Light & Fast

Functionalism

because form follows function

Integrity

because it's a living thing, not a high tech mess of code

UX first

because in the end it's all about the user
